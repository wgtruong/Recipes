Poke Bowl

Description

Poke is one of the best dishes that originally came from Hawaii. One of the biggest reasons why I love poke is because it uses raw fish.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds fresh tuna steaks, cubed
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 tablespoons seasame oil
  • 2 tablespoons finely chopped macaamia nuts
  • 1 tablespoon toasted seasame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon crushed red pepper(Optional)
  • Home made spicy sushi mayo

Steps

  1. Place tuna in a medium non-reactive bowl.
  2. Add soy sauce, green onions, seasame oil, seasame seeds, macadamia nuts, red pepper flakes, and spicy sushi mayo.
  3. Mix well then cover and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
