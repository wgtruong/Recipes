Spicy Sushi Mayo

Description

Spicy mayo is a delicious Asian recipe that goes really well with raw seafood! Honestly a super easy recipe to make at home!

This recipe goes really with with a Poke Bowl.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons Sriracha hot sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon seasame oil

Steps

  • Mix mayonnaise, Sriracha, and seasame oil together in a bowl using a fork until smooth.
