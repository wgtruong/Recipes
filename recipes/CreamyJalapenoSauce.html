Creamy Jalapeno Sauce

Description

Creamy Jalapeno Sauce is hands down the best recipe that is offered at Taco Bell!!! Now you can make this recipe at home!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons minced jalapenos, slices
  • 2 teaspoons jalapeno juice, from minced jalapenos
  • 3/4 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 dash salt

Steps

  • Combine sauce ingredients and stir until smooth.
